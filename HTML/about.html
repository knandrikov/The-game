About our team

We are team of 3 guys from Vocational school for computer programming and innovation in Burgas, Bulgaria.

To prepare us well for our future profession, our school gives us project activities through which we learn to work in a team, to allocate our time and also to show what we have learned and what we are capable of.

At the end of the year, the school set us the task of making a video game. Excited by the fact that for the first time we will make a working game, we started working.
